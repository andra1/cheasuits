# Additional Free Data Sources — St. Clair County, IL

Beyond lis pendens filings, these are untapped sources for finding distressed/undervalued residential properties.

---

## 1. Delinquent Tax Lists (Treasurer)

- **URL:** https://www.co.st-clair.il.us/departments/treasurer/tax-sale
- **Also:** https://www.co.st-clair.il.us/departments/treasurer/delinquent-real-estate
- **What:** Properties with unpaid property taxes. Published before annual tax sale (typically November).
- **Why it matters:** Many tax-delinquent owners never reach foreclosure. Different pipeline, often deeper distress.
- **Format:** Web-accessible; downloadable during sale season.
- **Cost:** Free

## 2. Tax Sale Auction Properties (Treasurer)

- **URL:** https://legacy.stclaircounty.org/Offices/treasurer/AuctionProperties.aspx
- **What:** Properties already through the tax sale process, listed for auction.
- **Why it matters:** Later stage of distress — often deeper discounts, motivated county disposition.
- **Cost:** Free

## 3. Recorder of Deeds — Liens & Quit-Claims (Fidlar)

- **URL:** https://www.co.st-clair.il.us/departments/recorder-of-deeds/remote-access
- **What:** Federal/state tax liens, quit-claim deeds, multiple mortgage recordings.
- **Why it matters:** Tax liens = IRS/state pressure on owner. Quit-claims often signal estate/divorce transfers. Multiple mortgages = over-leveraged.
- **Access:** Fidlar Technologies remote access (same system used for lis pendens).
- **Cost:** Free online access; copies $2/page.

## 4. Illinois MyDec Transfer Data (IDOR)

- **URL:** https://tax.illinois.gov/localgovernments/property/mydecdatafiles.html
- **Also:** https://data.illinois.gov/
- **What:** Real Estate Transfer Declarations — every property sale in Illinois since 2013. Weekly updates.
- **Why it matters:** Filter to St. Clair County for below-market sales, estate transfers, distressed pricing patterns. Can be used to build a local comp engine for ARV estimates.
- **Format:** Bulk CSV download.
- **Cost:** Free

## 5. HUD USPS Vacancy Data

- **URL:** https://www.huduser.gov/portal/datasets/usps.html
- **What:** Quarterly vacancy counts by Census tract. Addresses flagged vacant by USPS carriers (no mail collection for 90+ days).
- **Why it matters:** Neighborhood-level distress indicator. Properties in high-vacancy tracts score higher for distress.
- **Limitation:** Census tract level, not parcel-level.
- **Cost:** Free (registration required)

## 6. Circuit Court — Probate, Divorce, Bankruptcy (Jano Justice Systems)

- **URL:** https://www.co.st-clair.il.us/departments/circuit-clerk/courts/court-dockets
- **What:** Probate/estate filings, divorce cases with property, bankruptcy cases.
- **Why it matters:** Surfaces motivated sellers who haven't hit foreclosure yet. Inherited properties, divorce settlements, and bankruptcy liquidations are high-motivation leads.
- **Access:** Jano Justice Systems online docket search.
- **Cost:** Free

## 7. Municipal Code Violations

### East St. Louis
- **URL:** https://www.cesl.us/307/Code-Enforcement
- **Phone:** (618) 482-6000
- **What:** Abandoned houses, unsafe structures, demolition notices.

### Belleville
- **URL:** https://www.belleville.net/504/Code-of-Ordinances
- **Phone:** (618) 233-6810
- **What:** Code violations, building permits.

### St. Clair County Building & Zoning
- **URL:** https://www.co.st-clair.il.us/departments/building-zoning
- **Phone:** (618) 538-7700

- **Why it matters:** Condemned/violation properties often available cheap. Owners under municipal pressure to sell.
- **Access:** Requires FOIA requests or direct contact — no clean API.
- **Cost:** Free (FOIA)

## 8. Illinois Foreclosure Listing Services

- **IllinoisForeclosureList.com:** https://illinoisforeclosurelist.com/ — Daily sheriff sales & auctions, CSV/PDF/XLS downloads.
- **ILFLS.com:** https://ilfls.com — Extended foreclosures + probate listings with bulk download.
- **SheriffSales.net:** https://www.sheriffsales.net/listings/saint-clair-county-il/ — 6,400+ listings.
- **Cost:** Free (basic); some premium features.

## 9. HUD REO Inventory

- **URL:** https://www.hud.com/state/il
- **What:** HUD-owned foreclosure properties (FHA-backed or government-seized).
- **Why it matters:** Government-owned, often sold below market. Subset of all distressed properties.
- **Cost:** Free

## 10. Paid/Commercial (Future Consideration)

- **ATTOM Data API:** https://www.attomdata.com/ — Comps, AVMs, foreclosures, ownership, liens. Best quality for enrichment. Free 30-day trial.
- **Regrid:** https://app.regrid.com/us/il/st-clair — Parcel-level data with USPS vacancy overlays. Freemium.
- **BatchSkipTracing / IDI/TLO:** Owner contact info for outreach.
